Delivery Plan

A practical rollout framework to align business, technology, and operations on what happens first, who owns each milestone, and what must be ready before go-live.

30-60-90 day structure

Days 0-30: Scope and design
  • Finalize priority use cases
  • Confirm data and API dependencies
  • Define governance and escalation rules
Days 31-60: Build and validate
  • Implement workflows and integrations
  • Run QA, security checks, and UAT
  • Train operations and business owners
Days 61-90: Pilot and optimize
  • Controlled pilot release
  • KPI tracking and tuning cycles
  • Scale recommendation and executive review
Beyond 90 days: Scale
  • Expand channels and use cases
  • Operationalize governance and reporting
  • Transition to steady-state model

RACI starter (fill in owners)

  • Business owner: KPI definition and success criteria
  • Architecture lead: technical blueprint and constraints
  • Security owner: control validation and approval
  • Operations owner: monitoring, incident process, runbooks
  • Delivery manager: timeline, dependencies, escalation handling

Go-live gates

  • Functional and integration acceptance complete
  • Security controls signed off by relevant stakeholders
  • Support processes and fallback paths tested
  • Executive sign-off on pilot KPIs and risk posture
