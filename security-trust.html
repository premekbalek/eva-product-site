Security & Trust

A governance-first page for CTO, CISO, and compliance teams to capture controls, ownership, and evidence needed before procurement and deployment.

Shared responsibility model

Provider responsibilities
  • Platform-level security controls
  • Service monitoring and operational hardening
  • Incident response process and communication
Client responsibilities
  • Identity governance and access policy
  • Data classification and retention policy
  • Business process approvals and controls

Security due-diligence checklist

  • Data residency and transfer boundaries
  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • IAM model, roles, and privileged access controls
  • Audit logs and exportability for compliance evidence
  • Business continuity and recovery targets

Compliance mapping placeholders

Regulatory requirements
  • Industry-specific regulations
  • Internal policy mandates
  • Third-party audit expectations
Evidence artifacts
  • Security whitepaper and architecture diagram
  • Pen-test and vulnerability management summary
  • Control ownership matrix
