Finance page

Business Case

A structured template for CFO and transformation teams to build ROI and TCO with transparent assumptions. Replace placeholders with your operational baseline.

ROI model structure

Benefit drivers
  • Containment rate uplift (%)
  • Average handle time reduction (minutes)
  • Escalation rate reduction (%)
  • Revenue impact from proactive journeys
Cost drivers
  • Platform and service fees
  • Integration and implementation cost
  • Internal team effort allocation
  • Ongoing operations and model tuning

TCO checklist (24 months)

  • Direct software/service spend
  • Internal engineering and architecture cost
  • Data governance and security operations cost
  • Change-management and training cost
  • Scenario reserve for demand volatility

Scenario planning

Base case
  • Conservative automation assumptions
  • Standard rollout speed
  • Minimal scope expansion
Upside case
  • Faster adoption and higher containment
  • Multi-channel expansion in phase 2
  • Cross-functional reuse of assistants

Approval pack (to prepare)

  • Executive summary with assumptions and confidence ranges
  • One-page risk statement with mitigations
  • Rollout timeline linked to value milestones
  • Owner list for each KPI and cost line
